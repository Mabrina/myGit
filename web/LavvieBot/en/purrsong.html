Routine Updates

LavvieBot grows smarter through our regular firmware updates, keeping its features up to date with users’ feedback and changing needs.

Monitors Litter & Waste Levels

LavvieBot keeps you updated on its waste and litter levels on the "PurrSong" app (available on the App Store and Google Play).

1-Click Litter Ordering

Stock up on litter through the touch of a button. When litter levels are low, our app sends a notification and streamlines the process of purchasing litter.

Monitors Litter Box Usage

“PurrSong” keeps track of the frequency and length that your cat uses LavvieBot, so you can always stay on top of your cat’s health. LavvieBot can also recognize each cat in a multi-cat household if they weigh more than 1.5 pounds apart.

Customized Notifications

The notifications may be set to four different types, including Default, Cute, and Custom mode. Each personalized notification from your cat is sure to brighten up your day.

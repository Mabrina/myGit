Perfectly Automatic

Easy Maintenance

LavvieBot only needs to be cleaned every 2 to 3 weeks (for a one cat household), more than twice that of other automatic litter boxes thanks to LavvieBot’s proprietary auto-refilling system.

Auto-Cleans

LavvieBot’s developers worked for years to determine why other existing products often lead to malfunction, or leave behind waste during cleaning and cause odor. LavvieBot's patented rotating rake mechanism is designed to eliminate this issue.
Once the cat has left the machine, the separator scoops waste into the drawer at the interval of your choice (20 minutes to 1 hour). By focusing the rotational force of the motor onto the separator, LavvieBot’s mechanism demonstrates exceptional cleaning ability, preventing breakage and causing little noise.

 

1. Anti-Breakage
The separator’s design and placement prevent waste from reaching behind the cleaning area or sticking to the separator. Additionally, the increased contact area between separator and waste minimizes strain on the separator, ensuring that each gentle sift is meticulous and leaves no waste behind.

2. Non-Stick Litter Tray
FDA-approved PTFE coating prevents waste from sticking to the bottom of the tray.

Auto-Refills

LavvieBot refills litter from its 6.5L extra storage after cleaning, maintaining a fresh bed of litter for your cat every time.
